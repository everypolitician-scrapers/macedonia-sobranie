Alim Bilali 

 

Member of VMRO - DPMNE. Born on 11.8.1985 in Skudrinje. Macedonian Muslim. Medical High School „Nikola Stejn” - Tetovo.

 

Activities in the Assembly: 

  • Committee on Equal Opportunities for Women and Men, Member
  • Committee on Rules of Procedure and Mandatory and Immunity Issues, Member
  • Committee on Labour and Social Policy, Deputy Member
  • Committee on Local Self Governance, Deputy Member
  • Parliamentary Group of the Assembly of the Republic of Macedonia for Cooperation with the Parliament of  Islamic Republic of Iran, Member
  • Parliamentary Group of the Assembly of the Republic of Macedonia for Cooperation with the Parliament of  The Republic of Slovenia, Member
  • Parliamentary Group of the Assembly of the Republic of Macedonia for Cooperation with the Parliament of  Swiss Confederation, Member
